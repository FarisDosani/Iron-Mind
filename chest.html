Bench Press

Press a barbell or dumbbells upward while lying flat on a bench. This builds strength and mass in the chest, along with the triceps and shoulders.

Push-Ups

A bodyweight exercise where you lower and raise your body in a plank position. It improves chest strength and endurance while also engaging the core.

Incline Bench Press

Similar to the bench press, but performed on an incline to target the upper chest more effectively, along with the triceps and shoulders.

Chest Fly

Using dumbbells, open and close your arms in a wide arc while lying on a bench. This isolates the chest muscles, stretching and strengthening them.

Dips (Chest-Focused)

Perform dips on parallel bars with a slight forward lean to target the lower chest and triceps while also engaging the shoulders.
