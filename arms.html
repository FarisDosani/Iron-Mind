Bicep Curls (Dumbbell or Barbell)

A classic exercise to build biceps strength. Hold weights with palms facing forward, curl them towards your shoulders, then lower back down.

Tricep Dips

Using a bench or chair, lower your body by bending your elbows, targeting the triceps, then push back up to the starting position.

Hammer Curls

Similar to bicep curls but with palms facing each other throughout, working both biceps and forearms.

Overhead Tricep Extensions

Lift a dumbbell overhead, bend your elbows to lower it behind your head, then extend back up to target triceps.

Wrist Curls

Focus on forearm muscles by curling a dumbbell with your wrist while your forearm rests on your thigh.
