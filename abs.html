Plank

Hold your body straight in a forearm or high plank position. This builds core stability and strengthens the abs, lower back, and shoulders.

Crunches

Lie on your back, lift your shoulders off the ground, and contract your abs. This targets the upper abdominal muscles.

Bicycle Crunches

Alternate touching your elbow to the opposite knee in a pedaling motion while lying down. This engages both your upper abs and obliques.

Leg Raises

Lift your legs while lying on your back and lower them slowly. This exercise strengthens the lower abs.

Russian Twists

Twist your torso side-to-side while seated with your feet lifted. This targets the obliques and improves core rotation strength.
